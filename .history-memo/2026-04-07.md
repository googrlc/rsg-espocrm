## Screenshot 4: Create Account Modal — "show field based on account type" + "why is type just sitting there?"
**What's happening:** I found a **field naming mismatch** in the codebase:
- The **detail layout** (`detail.json`) uses `attribute: "type"` (the standard EspoCRM Account type field) to show/hide panels (Business Profile, Fleet & Operations, etc.)
- The **entityDefs** (`Account.json`) uses `attribute: "accountType"` (a custom field) for field-level visibility (fein, numberOfEmployees, etc.)
- These are **two different fields**. The "Type" field you see in the Create Account modal with a dash is the standard `type` field — it has no options configured to match your account types.

**The fix:** The `accountType` custom field (with options: Prospect, Commercial Lines, Personal Lines, Group Benefits, Medicare, Life Insurance) should be added to the Create Account modal layout, and it should drive the conditional field visibility. The standard `type` field is not being used meaningfully.
